wait — why is this great for ADHD brains? 🧠

because it was designed by someone with it who gets it. no clutter, no guilt, no shame — just vibes.

⚡ zero friction brain dumps
thought pops into your head? type it, tap it, or say it — Noto catches it before it vanishes. no forms, no folders, no fuss.
💡 AI that does the boring stuff for you
your notes are a mess? Cortex AI summarizes them, cleans up the formatting, explains things you forgot, and even quizzes you so it actually sticks. you just write — it organizes.
🎙️ just talk — it listens
can't type right now? hit record, talk it out, and Whisper transcribes it into a real note. perfect for shower thoughts and 2am ideas.
🔍 find things instantly
that note from three weeks ago? universal search finds it in milliseconds. even with voice input when typing feels like too much.
🎨 themes that spark joy
8 gorgeous themes because staring at the wrong color can ruin your whole day. dark, light, nord, dracula, tokyo night — pick your mood.
📱 works everywhere you go
iOS, Android, macOS, Windows, Linux. your notes are always with you, no matter which device you can find.
⭐ no overdue “organize” tasks
Noto doesn't guilt-trip you. no "you have 47 unsorted notes!" notifications. everything just works in the background.
🔒 peace of mind — your data stays yours
Noto is fully open source. your notes live on your device, not on some company's server. no tracking, no data harvesting, no “we may share your info with partners” fine print. when you use local AI, nothing ever leaves your machine. your brain is your business.
💰 100% free. forever. no catch.
no subscriptions, no paywalls, no premium tiers, no ads. Noto is completely free to use — because everyone deserves a second brain, not just people who can afford one.
